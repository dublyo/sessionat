About this data

Sessionat records the URL and title of every page you visit, the workspace it was in, and a timestamp — locally only. Nothing leaves your device. Visits older than 30 days are deleted automatically; you can also wipe everything anytime via the Wipe history button.

Incognito tabs are never tracked. Non-web URLs (chrome://, file://, etc.) are ignored.

🛡️

Your browsing stays on this device

Sessionat tracks every web page you visit — the URL, the title, the workspace it was in, and how long you spent on it — to power this dashboard. All of it stays on your computer. Nothing is sent to Sessionat or to any third party. Ever.

  • Incognito tabs are never tracked.
  • Non-web pages (chrome://, file://) are ignored.
  • Visits older than 30 days are deleted automatically.
  • You can wipe everything with one click via "Wipe history".
